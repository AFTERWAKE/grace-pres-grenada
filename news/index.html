Online Giving Available

You can now make an online donation to Grace Presbyterian Church! Grace Pres has teamed up with Tithe.ly, an online giving company, to allow you to give in whatever way you feel most comfortable. Tithe.ly allows you to give with your credit/debit card or directly with your bank account. You can set up recurring payments, and you can even pay with a text!
Visit the Giving Page to make an online donation or to learn more about how to give to Grace Presbyterian Church.
