At the pulpit

Chris Accardy started preaching at Grace in 2015 and was installed as the church’s regular pastor in 2016. He is also a registered nurse who works at a local hospital.

Chris started in ministry in 1991. He has held a variety of ministry positions since then. He holds bachelors degrees in nursing from the University of Memphis and medieval studies from Plymouth State University. He also holds a graduate certificate in human resource management from Southern New Hampshire University and a masters degree in pastoral ministry from Covenant Theological Seminary.

Pastor Chris’ main strengths are preaching & teaching, ministries of mercy & compassion, pastoral counseling, and mentoring & coaching 20-somethings.

Chris and his wife Shelley have been married since 1993 and have four young adult children and one grandchild. They love Grenada and the people who live here.

Behind the microphone

In December of 2017, Grace Presbyterian Church welcomed Reid Kelly as our new Worship Director.

Reid and his wife Lindsey are both teachers in the Grenada School District. Reid got his start leading worship for the North Greenwood Baptist youth group in Greenwood, Mississippi. After high school he attended Ole Miss and led worship for Reformed University Fellowship. For the last few years he has been a Worship Director at Westminster Presbyterian Church in Greenwood.

In the office

Honey Ledbetter is from the Philippine island Mindanao, in the southern part of the Philippines. She was born in the Municipality of Margosatubig, province of Zamboanga del Sur. Honey attended Misamis University in Ozamiz City Province of Missamis Oriental from 2006-2007 where she pursued a Bachelor of Arts in English. She then left school, and later attended North Central Mindanao Colleges in Municipality of Lala Province of Lanao del Norte where she pursued a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration and Marketing Management.
Honey got married in 2015, and now has two kids, Kyran and Daniel. She now works as the Office Manager at Grace Pres.
